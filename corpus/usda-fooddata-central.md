# FoodData Central

USDA’s comprehensive source of food composition data with multiple distinct data types

Search

Analytical data/metadata on commodity and minimally processed food samples.

**Source:** USDA

**Updates:** Twice annually - April & October

[Browse](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/food-search?type=Foundation)[Downloads](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/download-datasets)[Read More](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/Foundation_Foods_Documentation)

Historical data derived from analyses, calculations, and published literature.

**Source:** USDA

**Updates:** Final update was in 2018

[Browse](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/food-search?type=SR%20Legacy)[Downloads](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/download-datasets)[Read More](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/data-documentation)

Data applied to analyze foods/beverages reported in What We Eat in America, NHANES.

**Source:** USDA Foundation Foods & SR Legacy

**Updates:** Every 2 years

[Browse](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/food-search?type=Survey%20(FNDDS))[Downloads](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/download-datasets)[Read More](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/data-documentation)

Data from foods published in peer-reviewed journals in collaboration with USDA.

**Source:** Researchers

**Updates:** Twice annually (if available)

[Browse](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/food-search?type=Experimental)[Read More](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/Experimental_Foods_Documentation)

Label data collected through a public-private partnership.

**Source:** Commercial food brands

**Updates:** Monthly

[Browse](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/food-search?type=Branded)[Downloads](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/download-datasets)[Read More](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/GBFPD_Documentation)

 The USDA Global Branded Food Products Database (BFPDB) provided by the Global Data Synchronization Network (GDSN) is updated monthly. BFPDB no longer receives new and/or updated food component information from Label Insight after November 16, 2023. All food component information provided by Label Insight prior to and on this date will remain in the USDA Global Branded Food Products Database.

FNA Child Nutrition Database

The FNA Child Nutrition Database (CNDB) is now hosted on FDC! Find the CNDB and its related documents on the [CNDB page](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/cndb).

At this time, only a basic view of search results is available for viewing on mobile devices. Advanced filter features, such as searching by data type, are not yet mobile-enabled and are available only in desktop view. Users are encouraged to use a desktop computer to conduct food item searches.

USDA FoodData Central data are in the public domain and they are not copyrighted. They are published under [CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)

No permission is needed for their use, but we request that users list FoodData Central as the source of the data, and when possible, notify us of the product that uses the data so we may better track how FDC is being used in the public domain. The suggested citation is:
