## Historical Data on Crop Estimation

Satellite-based crop estimation in Pakistan provides comprehensive insights into the **area, production, and yield of major crops including wheat, sugarcane, rice, and cotton**. These estimates, generated through remote sensing analytics, help assess seasonal trends, provincial contributions, and the impacts of floods and climatic variability. The data supports **food security planning, agricultural policy formulation, and informed decision-making**, though the estimates are based on satellite analysis and are not field-validated.

### Wheat Estimation (Pakistan)

Wheat estimation in Pakistan is carried out using satellite data to assess cultivated area, crop health, and expected yield. These estimates support food security planning, import–export decisions, and timely policy formulation.
