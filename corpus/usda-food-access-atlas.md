# Food Access Research Atlas

The [Food Access Research Atlas](/data-products/food-access-research-atlas/go-to-the-atlas "Go to the Atlas"):

What can you do with the Atlas?

The [Food Access Research Atlas interactive guide](https://gisportal.ers.usda.gov/portal/apps/experiencebuilder/experience/?id=a53ebd7396cd4ac3a3ed09137676fd40 "Go to the FARA guide") provides an overview of the Food Access Research Atlas (FARA) to assist users in understanding its background information and its low-income and low-access measures.

[Go to the FARA guide](https://gisportal.ers.usda.gov/portal/apps/experiencebuilder/experience/?id=a53ebd7396cd4ac3a3ed09137676fd40)

***Note:*** Information about data sources, updates, and revisions to the data and mapping application is at [Documentation](/data-products/food-access-research-atlas/documentation "Documentation") and [Download the Data](/data-products/food-access-research-atlas/download-the-data "Download the Data").

[Enter the Atlas](/data-products/food-access-research-atlas/go-to-the-atlas "Food Access Research Atlas - Go to the Atlas")

The [Food Environment Atlas](/data-products/food-environment-atlas "Food Environment Atlas") provides users with a wide set of statistics on food choices, health and well-being, and community characteristics for all communities in the United States.
